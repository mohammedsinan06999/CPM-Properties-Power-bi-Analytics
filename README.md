# HR Analytics Dashboard

## Overview
This project presents an interactive HR Analytics Dashboard designed to analyze employee performance, promotion patterns, engagement levels, and attrition risk. The dashboard provides actionable insights to support data-driven HR decision-making and workforce planning.

By integrating multiple workforce indicators such as promotion eligibility, KPI performance, training scores, engagement levels, and absenteeism trends, the dashboard enables stakeholders to identify performance patterns, promotion dynamics, and potential retention risks within the organization.

## Business Objectives
The primary objectives of this analysis are:

- Evaluate whether promotion decisions align with employee eligibility and performance.
- Identify patterns in employee performance and development metrics.
- Analyze engagement levels and their relationship with attrition risk.
- Detect departments or groups with higher attrition risk.
- Provide insights to support strategic HR planning and employee retention initiatives.

## Dashboard Structure

### 1. Executive Summary
This page provides a high-level overview of workforce promotion and eligibility patterns.

Key elements include:
- Overall promotion rate
- Promotion distribution across departments and gender
- Eligibility versus promotion comparison
- Average attrition risk indicator

Purpose:
To provide leadership with a quick understanding of promotion dynamics and workforce risk indicators.

### 2. Performance & Development
This section focuses on the relationship between employee performance metrics and promotion outcomes.

Key analyses include:
- KPI score comparison between promoted and non-promoted employees
- Promotion distribution across performance categories (High, Average, Low)
- Training performance analysis across departments
- Overall KPI performance indicator

Purpose:
To evaluate whether promotions are aligned with employee performance and development outcomes.

### 3. Engagement & Attrition
This page analyzes workforce stability and potential attrition indicators.

Key analyses include:
- Relationship between engagement score and attrition risk
- Attrition risk distribution by department
- Attrition risk across salary bands
- Absenteeism trends
- Average employee service length

Purpose:
To identify early warning signals related to employee disengagement and potential turnover risk.

## Key Insights
The analysis highlights several important workforce trends:

- Promotion decisions show alignment with performance metrics, though additional factors influence final outcomes.
- Not all eligible employees receive promotions, indicating competition and role availability constraints.
- Engagement levels demonstrate a relationship with attrition risk, suggesting that engagement initiatives may support retention.
- Certain departments display higher attrition risk, which may require targeted HR interventions.
- Absenteeism and engagement indicators can serve as early signals of workforce dissatisfaction.
